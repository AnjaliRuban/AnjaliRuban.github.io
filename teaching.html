I am currently a TA for CS 61B (the best class at Berkeley). Spring 2021 will be my fourth time on course staff and my third time as TA. Teaching online is a very different experience from teaching in person, but I hope that we can all work together to make this as good as a normal semester.

In Fall 2020, I was the discussion TA, so if you’d like to see my walkthrough videos, they are all in a playlist here. If you’d like to set up an appointment to talk to me about anything (61B related or otherwise) please email me and I’ll get back to you as soon as possible.

Prior to coming to Berkeley, I was a teacher’s aide for Pleasanton Unified School District’s elementary summer school, working primarily with second graders and children with mild/moderate disabilities. I also taught Tamil for three years at the California Tamil Academy.
