I am interested in how abstractness and grounding can be used to make language models more flexible when it comes to dialectal differences. I am a heritage speaker of Sri Lankan Tamil, a dialect that is in danger of disappearing due to half a century of persecution, so making systems that don’t further alienate speakers of uncommon dialects is very close to my heart.
